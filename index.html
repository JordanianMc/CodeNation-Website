HololiveEN

HololiveEN is the english branch of the VTuber agency Hololive productions

Hololive Productions is a VTuber agency that consists of the talent groups Hololive and HOLOSTARS. hololive productions has over 70 affiliated talents active in Japan, Indonesia, and English-speaking countries, and has over 70 million subscribers across all of their Youtube channels.

Using COVER Corporation's VR/AR technology, hololive productions talents provide various experiences ranging from game streams to offline concerts, and form a stable community that connects fans, creators, and talents all around the world via social networks.
